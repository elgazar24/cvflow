About CVFlow

CVFlow is an intelligent CV generation platform that helps you create professional, tailored resumes in minutes. Our AI-powered system provides personalized recommendations and ensures your CV stands out in today's competitive job market.

Our Mission

To empower job seekers with innovative tools that streamline the resume creation process and increase their chances of landing their dream jobs through personalized, data-driven recommendations.

Our Vision

To revolutionize how people create and optimize their professional documents, making career advancement accessible to everyone through intuitive design and artificial intelligence.

Key Features

AI Resume Builder

Generate professional resumes instantly from your JSON data with smart formatting and structure recommendations.

Smart Content Suggestions

Get personalized recommendations for skills, objectives, and achievements that match your career goals.

Customizable Templates

Choose from a variety of professional templates and customize colors, fonts, and layouts to match your personal style.

User Dashboard

Manage all your CV data, track changes, and update your professional information from one convenient location.
